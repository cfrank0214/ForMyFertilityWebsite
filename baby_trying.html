Trying to have a baby?

You are not alone. If you've been trying for a baby for a year or more, it might be time to see your doctor- if you are over 35 and been trying to conceive for over 6 months, time to seek medical advice. You doctor my suggest a fertility specialist and assisted reproductive treatment. (link to ART site)
