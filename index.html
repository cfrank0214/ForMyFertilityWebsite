Age

Women's fertility starts to gradually decline around age 27 and drops off dramatically after age 35.

Men

Not just a women’s issue-infertility affects men and women equally. Sperm count is an important factor in a man’s fertility status. Studies show that the average sperm count dropped significantly and age plays a factor- after age 40 fewer healthy sperm are produced.
